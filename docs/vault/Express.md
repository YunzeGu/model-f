# Express

One of 6 drives in the [[Drive System]].

**The urge to externalize — move, vocalize, create.**

## Hormone Weights

- [[Dopamine]] +0.8 (motivation to create/output)
- [[Norepinephrine]] +0.7 (arousal energy)
- [[Cortisol]] -0.5 (stress inhibits expression)

## When It's High

The agent wants to *output* something — move its body, make sounds, create. This is the externalization dimension.

## Related Emotions ([[Emotion Map]])

- High → "excited", "energized", "restless"
