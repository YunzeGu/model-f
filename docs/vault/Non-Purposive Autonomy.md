# Non-Purposive Autonomy

The core design goal of [[Model F]].

## The Idea

Current AI is **goal-oriented**: given objective → optimize execution. [[Model F]] rejects this at the surface level.

**Model F gives machines the desire to act without a reason.**

## Two-Layer Structure

- **Deep layer**: Evolutionary-analog rules ([[Homeostatic Decay]], [[Circadian Rhythm]], [[Cross-Hormone Interactions]]) provide energy and directional tendency
- **Surface layer**: Emergent [[Impulse|impulses]] that appear purposeless — wanting to move, create, explore, rest, for no specific reason

The deep rules **fuel** behavior but don't **dictate** it.

## The Gap Is Autonomy

The space between "cortisol is high" and "the agent withdraws" is where autonomy lives. The mapping is probabilistic and noisy, not deterministic.

## Biological Analogue

Humans have "survive/reproduce" at the deep level, but at the surface level they stare at clouds, read philosophy at 3 AM, and rearrange furniture for no reason. The deep pressure is real, but the surface expression is free.
