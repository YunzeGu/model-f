# Engage

One of 6 drives in the [[Drive System]].

**Readiness to act, initiate interaction.**

## Hormone Weights

- [[Norepinephrine]] +1.0 (arousal is the primary driver)
- [[Dopamine]] +0.6 (motivation facilitates engagement)
- [[Cortisol]] -0.3 (stress inhibits engagement)

## When It's High

The agent is ready to act on whatever drive is strongest. This is the "energy to do" dimension — it doesn't specify *what* to do, just the readiness to do *something*.

## Related Emotions ([[Emotion Map]])

- High → "energized", "excited"
- Low → "lethargic"
