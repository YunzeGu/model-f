# Cortisol

One of 6 hormones in the [[Hormone System]].

- **Setpoint:** 0.3 (low baseline — stress is not the default state)
- **Decay rate:** 0.02 (very slow — stress lingers)
- **Circadian amplitude:** 0.15 (strongest circadian effect — peaks in "morning")
- **Role:** Stress, alertness, fight-or-flight arousal

## Behavioral Effects via [[Drive System]]

- High cortisol → strong [[Withdraw]] and [[Seek Comfort]] drives
- High cortisol → suppresses [[Engage]] and [[Express]]

## Interactions ([[Cross-Hormone Interactions]])

- Suppresses [[Serotonin]] (-0.3) — stress kills contentment
- Raises [[Norepinephrine]] (+0.2) — stress increases alertness
- Suppressed by [[Endorphin]] (-0.2) and [[Oxytocin]] (-0.1)
