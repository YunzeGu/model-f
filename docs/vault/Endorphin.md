# Endorphin

One of 6 hormones in the [[Hormone System]].

- **Setpoint:** 0.3
- **Decay rate:** 0.02 (slow — endorphin effects linger)
- **Role:** Pain suppression, euphoric contentment, inertia

## Behavioral Effects via [[Drive System]]

- High endorphin → strong [[Rest]] drive (euphoric stillness)

## Interactions ([[Cross-Hormone Interactions]])

- Suppresses [[Cortisol]] (-0.2) — endorphins counteract stress
