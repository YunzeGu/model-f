# Seek Novelty

One of 6 drives in the [[Drive System]].

**The urge to explore, find new stimulation.**

## Hormone Weights

- [[Dopamine]] +1.2 (primary driver)
- [[Serotonin]] -0.8 (contentment suppresses novelty-seeking)
- [[Norepinephrine]] +0.3 (arousal facilitates exploration)

## When It's High

The agent wants to explore, investigate, find something new. In biological terms: curiosity, restlessness, boredom-driven exploration.

## When It's Low

The agent is satisfied with current state. No urge to seek.

## Related Emotions ([[Emotion Map]])

- High → "curious", "restless", "excited"
- Low → "content", "calm"
