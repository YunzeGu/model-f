# Serotonin

One of 6 hormones in the [[Hormone System]].

- **Setpoint:** 0.6 (highest baseline — contentment is the default)
- **Decay rate:** 0.03 (slow — mood shifts gradually)
- **Role:** Contentment, satiation, inhibition of impulsive behavior

## Behavioral Effects via [[Drive System]]

- High serotonin → strong [[Rest]] drive, suppresses [[Seek Novelty]]
- Low serotonin → restlessness, increased novelty-seeking

## Interactions ([[Cross-Hormone Interactions]])

- Slightly suppresses [[Dopamine]] (-0.15)
- Suppressed by high [[Cortisol]] (-0.3) — stress overrides contentment
