# Floating Brain

A consciousness model with **no sensory organs**, suspended in a void, driven entirely by internal dynamics.

## Why This Scope

- A [[3D Embodied Simulation]] (e.g., cat in Unity) requires solving sensory decoding first — too complex
- A [[LLM Dialogue]] mode requires rational reasoning — but even simple animals have desires without logic
- Starting with the most minimal viable unit: pure internal dynamics → spontaneous [[Impulse|impulses]]

## What It Means

- No vision, no touch, no hearing
- All state changes come from [[Hormone System|internal hormone dynamics]] (decay, noise, circadian rhythm, cross-interactions)
- The brain "breathes" on its own — [[Circadian Rhythm]] creates day/night cycles, [[Biological Noise]] creates moment-to-moment variation
