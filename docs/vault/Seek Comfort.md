# Seek Comfort

One of 6 drives in the [[Drive System]].

**The urge to find safety, warmth, social proximity.**

## Hormone Weights

- [[Oxytocin]] +1.0 (bonding urge is the primary driver)
- [[Cortisol]] +0.5 (stress amplifies comfort-seeking)
- [[Serotonin]] -0.3

## When It's High

The agent seeks warmth, safety, proximity to others. Combination of bonding desire and stress-driven need for reassurance.

## Related Emotions ([[Emotion Map]])

- High → "anxious" (if stress-driven), "content" (if oxytocin-driven)
