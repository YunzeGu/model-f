# Norepinephrine

One of 6 hormones in the [[Hormone System]].

- **Setpoint:** 0.4
- **Decay rate:** 0.04
- **Role:** Arousal, attention, readiness for action

## Behavioral Effects via [[Drive System]]

- High norepinephrine → strong [[Engage]] and [[Express]] drives
- Low norepinephrine → [[Rest]] drive increases, [[Withdraw]] may increase

## Interactions ([[Cross-Hormone Interactions]])

- Raised by high [[Cortisol]] (+0.2) and [[Dopamine]] (+0.1)
- Slightly raises [[Dopamine]] (+0.05)
