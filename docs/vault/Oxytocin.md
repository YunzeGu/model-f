# Oxytocin

One of 6 hormones in the [[Hormone System]].

- **Setpoint:** 0.4
- **Decay rate:** 0.03
- **Role:** Social bonding urge, comfort-seeking, proximity desire

## Behavioral Effects via [[Drive System]]

- High oxytocin → strong [[Seek Comfort]] drive

## Interactions ([[Cross-Hormone Interactions]])

- Slightly suppresses [[Cortisol]] (-0.1) — social comfort reduces stress
