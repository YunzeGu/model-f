# Dopamine

One of 6 hormones in the [[Hormone System]].

- **Setpoint:** 0.5
- **Decay rate:** 0.05 (fast — dopamine spikes are short-lived)
- **Role:** Reward anticipation, novelty-seeking, motivation to act

## Behavioral Effects via [[Drive System]]

- High dopamine → strong [[Seek Novelty]], [[Engage]], [[Express]] drives
- Low dopamine → resistance to action, tendency toward low-cost stimulation (like doomscrolling)

## Interactions ([[Cross-Hormone Interactions]])

- Slightly raises [[Norepinephrine]] (+0.1)
- Suppressed by high [[Serotonin]] (-0.15) — the "satisfied, less seeking" effect

## Real-World Analogue

Dopamine detox phenomenon: chronic overstimulation depletes dopamine → procrastination and compulsive short-reward seeking. Recovery restores motivation and action drive.
