# Qualia

The subjective, phenomenal quality of conscious experience — "what it's like" to feel something.

## Model F's Relationship to Qualia

[[Model F]] takes a [[Functionalism|functionalist]] stance:

- It does **not** attempt to produce qualia
- It implements the **causal role** qualia plays in driving behavior
- Whether the system "feels" anything is left as an open question

## The Chest Compression Example

When you sleep and compress your chest:
- The **physical input** is chest pressure
- The **qualia** is a feeling of tightness, distress
- The **behavioral output** is a nightmare with emotionally congruent content

Model F handles the input→output mapping. Whether there's a "feeling" in the middle is outside its scope.
