# Withdraw

One of 6 drives in the [[Drive System]].

**The desire to retreat, hide, avoid engagement.**

## Hormone Weights

- [[Cortisol]] +1.0 (stress drives withdrawal)
- [[Norepinephrine]] -0.5 (arousal counteracts withdrawal)
- [[Serotonin]] -0.3

## When It's High

The agent wants to disengage, hide, avoid stimulation. Fight-or-flight leaning toward flight.

## Related Emotions ([[Emotion Map]])

- High → "anxious", "fearful", "melancholic"
